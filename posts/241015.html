TwoSetViolin

OCT 15 2024

I remember watching TwoSet marvel over Sohyun Ko playing Zigeunerweisen, showing my mom how a 12-year-old girl was better at violin than these two guys. And how my mom gave me a reality check by saying that Brett and Eddy were better than me.

I remember thinking that their Asian mom skits were the funniest videos ever. I shared them with my friends at the time and we tried to imitate the accent.

I remember binging their videos when I was a sad kid during distance learning: their old skits, violin charades, Ling Ling workouts, the lofi making video, and the violin vs bass battle. I was starting to become bored of playing the violin, but TwoSet motivated me to practice more often.

I remember tuning in to Eddy's Sibelius 3 mil livestream with my family after online school ended that day. It was probably the only time I have ever watched anything on YouTube together with my mom.

I thought it was hilarious when my non-Twosetter friend realized that TwoSetViolin was called TwoSetViolin because it was two guys who both played the violin.

Since my parents weren't really into classical music, I wouldn't have known anything if it weren't for TwoSet. When my teacher gave me the sheet music for Sarasate's Introduction and Tarantella last month, it felt so familiar that I thought I had to have played the piece before. But actually, it was familiar because I heard it over and over again in TwoSet videos.

And thanks to TwoSet, interesting is the first adjective that comes to my mind.

Yesterday morning, I came across TwoSet's last Instagram post. I was quite confused, since it was so sudden and I just had one of their recent videos recommended to me a few days before. And didn't they also prank us in a similar way for April Fools? I was not so convinced that TwoSet was quitting. Besides, r/lingling40hrs further fueled my false hopes and convinced me that they were just rebranding.

I wish their videos weren't taken down, as I would love to be watching them now. But unfortunately, we have to move on.
