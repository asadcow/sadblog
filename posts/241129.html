Annoying Things - the rest

NOV 29 2024

I have a really long list of things that I don't like, separated into categories. For this post, I removed some of the ones that are commonly considered annoying or would require too much explanation. (Making really long lists in HTML is painful. It's a new li tag for each of them, while this entire paragraph is just one p tag) As I was editing, I removed enough so that the remaining three categories could fit in one post. Some of the things can be in multiple categories, but ironically, I don't think that's too big of a deal. So here is the list.

first part: School

(English) language

  • common phrases: “make it or break it” “I'm sick of it” “practice makes perfect!” “sky high”
  • the acronym HAGS (have a great summer)
  • mixing up your and you're, bought and brought, and other similar words
  • unnecessary/incorrect apostrophes in plural words (my post's are so cool)
  • possessives for a name ending in S: James' books (wrong) vs James's books (correct)
  • using “all in all” or “to sum it up” as transitions
  • when people get 9th place and say they got top 9 instead of top 10 or 9th
  • first of all, second of all, third of all, (one minute later) tenth of all, especially when the tenth reason almost reverse proves your point
  • people only using the word “cute” to describe their things
  • when people older than 10 years old say they “play” with their friends

YouTube

  • when people do “What's In My Backpack” videos, but they put stationery in their backpacks without taking it out of the packaging
  • storytime as a noun incorrectly (“i have a storytime”)
  • the stupid open mouth thumbnail face
  • when people edit their comments to say “ty for likes”
  • censored spoilers in thumbnails but you can still see through it
  • when a YouTuber says “24-hour challenge,” but they end the challenge before they sleep so the challenge is less than 24 hours

Other

  • when people put (string instrument) bow rosin away without folding the cloth neatly
  • when golfers put tees in their hair (THE TEE IS DIRTY WHY WOULD YOU PUT IT IN YOUR HAIR unless you wash your hair every day BUT WHY WOULD YOU WASH YOUR HAIR EVERY DAY)
  • when everybody sings happy birthday in a different key
  • when songs are sped up for tiktok and the singer sounds like a chipmunk
  • people who play card games but the cards are barely even in a stack
  • when people play paper.io but don't fill all the way to the edge
  • when Minecraft builders build chimneys but there isn't a fireplace inside
  • when girls know how to do their own makeup but not their hair
  • when my parents don't close my bedroom door the way I want it to be closed at night
  • when you're forced to put an item away on a shelf it doesn't belong on while shopping
