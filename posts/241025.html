NEW LIFE SERIES

OCT 26 2024

Warning: spoilers. And if you have never watched Grian's life series, Hermitcraft, or anything similar, you will have no idea what any of this means.

today, saturday, october 19, at about 8:45 am PST, i found smallishbeans's new youtube video, and i saw “wild life” in the title. interesting. and i accidentally spoiled it for myself with a comment that mentioned how bdubs was the first one to find out about the shrinking. i had no idea what that meant, but… interesting.

after the initial introduction, i thought the series was gonna be like afterlife smp where you were randomly assigned an origin for each life. but i think the actual mechanic is a lot more interesting and unique. i'm always impressed by how grian comes up with the concepts.

joel, gem, and bdubs were in a forest when they discovered the wildcard. bdubs shrunk to a size even shorter than one block, it was funny how he said he was “sinking” while it happened. and i was a bit scared since when they tried to jump to unshrink, there was no noticeable difference. but later in the video, lizzie was absolutely massive after jumping, so it's definitely possible to unshrink.

i also think it's interesting that they had 6 lives. i wonder if some of the wildcards in later episodes will make them more prone to taking damage? because why else would they need 6?

i'm really excited for the joel and gem team. i enjoyed watching them team at the beginning of real life (there's an animation of joel immensely struggling with VR and i watched that many times) and everything sort of fell apart when they split. so i hope they stick together in this series and win.

i used to keep up with all of minecraft's updates (like since 2021) and i knew a lot of minecraft mechanics. but i didn't watch minecraft live this year. so when they went into the pale forest or whatever it's called, i had no idea what they were trying to do. i just knew that there was this creature that has to be killed in a strange way and you have to be constantly looking at it.

so now it is a few days later, and i have been watching grian's episode. i really like sub 1 club's vibes and it was funny when they went mining and kept taking damage. but what is their team name for the next episode? surely it won't be another size wildcard.

i'll just say that i'm definitely not gonna write a post every single week. but i'm planning to write about wild life after it ends. and the next post will either be about school or babymonster's new album when it releases. yay
